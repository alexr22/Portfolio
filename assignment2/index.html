About Me

I am 23 years old and have lived in Austin my entire life. I went to Austin High School and then studied Petroleum Engineering at the University of Texas. After graduating in August, 2015, I worked on an oil rig for six months, which was quite the experience. I hope to go back to the oil and gas industry after I complete the UT Coding Boot Camp. It would be cool to take what I learn from this course and apply it to that industry. I am currently working for Reed Insurance Agency and taking classes at night and on the weekends.

I grew up as the youngest of four children, with two older brothers and an oldest sister. We loved sports and competing throughout our childhood. My family is rapidly growing as I have a niece and three nephews. When I am not working or coding, I enjoy hanging out with friends and family, whether it be playing sports, playing video games, going out to eat, or just hanging out.
